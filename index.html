I therefore chose 4 associations among many that deserve to be better known.

Action Against Hunger

In 2016, ACF provided food aid to 5.1 million people in 49 countries.

Fondation hôpitaux de Paris-Hôpitaux de France

An association that collects donations to help hospitals during the covid 19 crisis.

Médecins sans frontières

51% of projects (98.9 million euros) targeted victims of armed conflict in 2016.

Petits Princes

A very nice association that makes 494 sick children's dreams come true (+11% in one year) in 150 pediatric departments in France, with the help of 88 volunteers compared to 78 a year earlier.
